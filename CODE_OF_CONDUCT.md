# Code of Conduct

This project should be a practical and respectful place to work.

## Expected behavior

- Be direct and considerate.
- Focus criticism on ideas, code, and behavior.
- Assume public comments will be read by people with different levels of context.
- Keep security discussions careful and specific.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Abusive language aimed at a person or group.
- Deliberately disrupting issues, pull requests, or discussions.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, close issues, block users, or take other reasonable action to protect the project.
